OPUS Tuition Money-Back Guarentee

OPUS Tuition guarantees that, within a 12-month period, the Student will achieve at least a 10 percent improvement in their academic results.

Expectations

Parents and guardians can expect a personalized and supportive learning environment where the Student can thrive, alongside clear and regular communication regarding their academic progress.

Refund Policy

If the Student does not meet the specified improvement benchmark, OPUS Tuition will process a full refund of the tuition fees paid for the year, ensuring that the investment in the student's education is risk-free.

Terms and Conditions of the Results Guarantee

To qualify for the Results Guarantee, the following conditions must be adhered to:

  1. Homework Completion: The Student must complete all assigned homework, allowing for up to 3 missed assignments per quarter or per 12 lessons.
  2. Full Program Commitment: The Student must complete a total of 48 lessons (1 lesson per week, 2 hours each) over the course of one year.
  3. Improvement Calculation: Academic improvement will be assessed as a trend, calculated through the average marks of school-issued Weighted Assessments (WAs) and personal lesson topical tests. If, after one year, there is no overall increase of at least 10 marks, a full refund will be processed.
